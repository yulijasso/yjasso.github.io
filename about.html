ABOUT ME

Greetings! I'm Yuliana Denisse Jasso, a dedicated Computer Scientist on a journey shaped by unique experiences and unyielding passion.

My academic voyage commenced in an unexpected realm - I embarked on my undergraduate studies as a major in Computer Information Systems within the Business School. Immersed in the intricacies of business, I delved into marketing strategies, business plans, and the art of HR management. Yet, it was the pivotal moment when I ventured into my core major classes of Computer Information Systems that my trajectory shifted. Introducing myself to the realm of programming through my first class, "Intro to Business Programming," I was captivated. However, my disappointment ensued upon realizing it stood as the sole programming course within the CIS curriculum. It was in this juncture that the realization dawned upon me - to align my academic pursuit with my true passion: Computer Science. Driven by an unwavering fervor, I embraced the challenge, even if it meant delaying my graduation. The allure of Computer Science was magnetic, igniting a spark within me unlike any before. With each passing day, my dedication reflected in my academic performance, reaffirming the adage that true excellence thrives when passion fuels endeavor.

Four years later, I proudly stand as a graduate holding a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - proof that pursuing one's passion yields not just success, but profound fulfillment.
